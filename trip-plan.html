What do we do with the days in the middle?

Both plans keep the Budapest bookends identical — only 18–26 July changes. Flip between them, or read side by side.

Option A

The Hungary Roadtrip

Rent a car and loop the country — Lake Balaton wine country, Mediterranean Pécs, the cellars of Eger, the bend of the Danube — then roll back into Budapest for Charlie Puth. No flights, no airports, all road.

19 JulSun🛏 Balaton

To Lake Balaton 🚗

Pick up the car, drive to the north shore (~1.5h). Tihany peninsula — lavender, abbey, lakeside sunset and wine.

20 JulMon🛏 Balaton

Lake day

Badacsony wine hills, a long beach afternoon, maybe a boat. Slow and sunny.

21 JulTue🛏 Pécs

South to Pécs 🚗

~2h drive. Zsolnay Cultural Quarter, the early-Christian Cella Septichora, a café crawl in the warmest, most Mediterranean city in Hungary.

22 JulWed🛏 Eger

Pécs → Eger 🚗

Baroque town under a hilltop castle. Evening in the wine cellars of the Valley of the Beautiful Women.

23 JulThu🛏 Eger

Tokaj day trip

~1.5h to the famous sweet-wine region and back — or a slow do-nothing day in Eger if we'd rather.

24 JulFri🛏 Danube Bend

West to the Danube Bend 🚗

Drive toward the river's elbow. Szentendre — cobbled, artsy, full of little galleries and ice cream.

25 JulSat🛏 Budapest

Castles & basilicas

Visegrád citadel and the vast Esztergom Basilica over the Danube, then roll back into Budapest.

26 JulSun🛏 Budapest

Margaret Island, one more bath, dessert at Nándori. Nothing on the clock.
