## Live decision — checked Wednesday 22 July

The seven-night routes below are reference drafts for a **Thursday 23 July** departure. The realistic window before Monday's Budapest anchor is **23–26 July: four days / three nights**.

Best fit for 23–26 July: one Balaton base, with **Zamárdi Adventure Park, Tihany, Tapolca Lake Cave and Szigliget Castle**, then **Hévíz or a slow lake day** before returning to Budapest. Kapolcs is lively rather than quiet once Valley of Arts begins on Friday 24 July. Pécs and the complete north-east loop need a later or longer trip.

**Crowd strategy:** leave Budapest early Thursday rather than arriving Friday evening. Put Zamárdi on Thursday and Tihany first thing Friday; protect Saturday with a timed Tapolca ticket plus late Szigliget, then keep Sunday flexible for Hévíz at opening or the return drive.

## Loop A — Lake, spa & arts

**Route:** Budapest → Pécs → North Balaton → Budapest

**Pace:** relaxed · 2 road-trip bases · about 6–7 hours of relocation driving plus local outings

**Best for:** swimming, thermal recovery, cave boating, cafés, culture and summer-festival atmosphere.

**Wine:** not required.

| Date | Sleep | Plan |
|---|---|---|
| **19 Jul** | Pécs | Leave early; Cella Septichora and Vasarely Museum while open on Sunday, then cafés. |
| **20 Jul** | Pécs | Zsolnay Quarter's summer-Monday exhibitions, Ottoman old town and Mecsek sunset. |
| **21 Jul** | North Balaton | Move to one lake base for five nights; swim and unpack. |
| **22 Jul** | North Balaton | Timed Tapolca Lake Cave boat ride + Szigliget Castle. |
| **23 Jul** | North Balaton | Hévíz thermal lake or a beach-and-cycle recovery day. |
| **24 Jul** | North Balaton | Tihany + opening day of Művészetek Völgye around Kapolcs. |
| **25 Jul** | North Balaton | Festival, Káli Basin or a completely slow lake day. |

**Main trade-off:** less dramatic hiking and fewer castle-quest days; Balaton is busy in late July.

## Loop B — Caves, castles & forests

**Route:** Budapest → Eger/Bükk → Aggtelek → Zemplén → Budapest

**Pace:** active · 3 road-trip bases · about 8–10 hours of relocation driving plus day trips

**Best for:** huge caves, castle ruins, forest railways, hiking and adventure activities.

**Wine:** Eger and Tokaj wine stops are optional and can be skipped entirely.

| Date | Sleep | Plan |
|---|---|---|
| **19 Jul** | Eger | Castle casemates, old town, minaret and thermal soak. |
| **20 Jul** | Eger | Lillafüred/Bükk: forest railway, Hámori Lake, waterfall and show cave. |
| **21 Jul** | Eger | Szalajka Valley, narrow-gauge railway and optional Eger bath recovery. |
| **22 Jul** | Aggtelek | Timed Baradla dripstone-cave tour and a quiet national-park night. |
| **23 Jul** | Zemplén | Boldogkő Castle and Sárospatak, then settle into one Zemplén base. |
| **24 Jul** | Zemplén | Pálháza forest railway + Füzér or Regéc fortress. |
| **25 Jul** | Zemplén | Megyer-hegy Tarn, chairlift, bobsled or zipline. |

**Main trade-off:** more unpacking, timed reservations and rural driving; quieter evenings.

## Decision rule

- Choose **Loop A** if “holiday” means recovery, lake time and summer atmosphere.
- Choose **Loop B** if it means collecting caves, castles and small adventures.
