## 🚗 Option A — Hungary roadtrip

- **17 Jul (Fri)** — Vienna → Budapest. City Park pre-show stroll: Heroes' Square · Vajdahunyad Castle · Ferris Wheel · Musical Fountain. 🎤 **Valmar**. Dinner: Retro Lángos / Pirate Empire. 🛏 Budapest
- **18 Jul (Sat)** — Budapest: Rudas or Széchenyi thermal bath, Citadel + Gellért Hill view, Buda Castle. Dinner: for sale pub. *(optional: SPARTY bath party)* 🛏 Budapest
- **19 Jul (Sun)** — Pick up car → **Lake Balaton** north shore (~1.5h). Tihany peninsula: lavender, abbey, lakeside sunset + wine. 🛏 Balaton
- **20 Jul (Mon)** — Balaton: Badacsony wine hills, beach/boat day. 🛏 Balaton
- **21 Jul (Tue)** — Drive to **Pécs** (~2h). Zsolnay Quarter, Cella Septichora, café crawl. 🛏 Pécs
- **22 Jul (Wed)** — Pécs → **Eger** (~3h). Eger Castle; evening wine cellars at Valley of the Beautiful Women. 🛏 Eger
- **23 Jul (Thu)** — Day trip **Tokaj** (~1.5h) sweet-wine tasting — *or* slow day in Eger. 🛏 Eger
- **24 Jul (Fri)** — Eger → **Danube Bend**. Szentendre artsy old town. 🛏 Szentendre/Visegrád
- **25 Jul (Sat)** — Visegrád citadel + Esztergom Basilica → back toward Budapest. 🛏 Budapest
- **26 Jul (Sun)** — Return car. Slow Budapest: Margaret Island, baths, Nándori Cukrászda dessert. 🛏 Budapest
- **27 Jul (Mon)** — Last pins (Hopp Ferenc Museum / Budapest Jazz Club). 🎤 **Charlie Puth**. 🛏 Budapest
- **28 Jul (Tue)** — Budapest → Vienna. 🏁

> ✂️ *Too much driving? Drop Tokaj or Pécs and add a 3rd Balaton night for a slower, more romantic pace.*

## 🇬🇧 Option B — London week

- **17 Jul (Fri)** — Vienna → Budapest. City Park pre-show stroll. 🎤 **Valmar**. 🛏 Budapest
- **18 Jul (Sat)** — Budapest morning (quick bath/stroll) → afternoon flight **BUD→London** (~2.5h). Check in. Evening: Soho / Covent Garden / Chinatown. 🛏 London
- **19 Jul (Sun)** — Westminster Abbey, Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, St James's Park, Trafalgar Sq. 🛏 London
- **20 Jul (Mon)** — British Museum, Sir John Soane's, Camden Market, Primrose Hill sunset, Regent's Park. 🛏 London
- **21 Jul (Tue)** — City: Tower Bridge, Sky Garden, Borough Market, The Shard, St Paul's, Barbican Conservatory. 🛏 London
- **22 Jul (Wed)** — South Kensington: Natural History + Science Museum, Harrods, Notting Hill + Portobello Road. 🛏 London
- **23 Jul (Thu)** — Greenwich day via Uber Boat + Cable Car: Maritime Museum, Royal Observatory. Platform 9¾ at King's Cross on the way back. 🛏 London
- **24 Jul (Fri)** — Day trip: **Windsor Castle** (or Oxford). Evening: West End show / Cahoots cocktail bar. 🛏 London
- **25 Jul (Sat)** — Last London morning (Sky Garden / Harrods) → evening flight **London→BUD**. 🛏 Budapest
- **26 Jul (Sun)** — Budapest recovery: thermal bath, Citadel, easy day. 🛏 Budapest
- **27 Jul (Mon)** — Budapest. 🎤 **Charlie Puth**. 🛏 Budapest
- **28 Jul (Tue)** — Budapest → Vienna. 🏁

> ✈️ *~6.5 days in London. Book the BUD↔LON flights early for mid-budget fares; late-July London hotels are the big cost line.*
